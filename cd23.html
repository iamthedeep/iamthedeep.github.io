3D PRINTING

Cetus3D recently launched their super minimalist, high resolution 3D printer on Kickstarter. The very cool thing is that early birds can pick this up for only $199. Would love to try one out.

https://www.kickstarter.com/projects/854865943/cetus3d-at-199-get-all-you-need-to-start-3d-printi

FUTURE FARMING

CNH Industrial unveiled their autonomous tractor concept, basically freeing up farmers, and removing more future jobs.

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=T7Os5Okf3OQ

senseFly also showed off their eBee SQ farming drone which can autonomously survey and analyse fields using it's multispectral camera technology.

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wtjqFtOvtIs

VIRTUAL REALITY

Some MIT students have been testing out giving VR headsets to seniors living in a Brookdale care home, and it's blowing their minds. Pretty cool.

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=e6LgI4z0-c4

In other news, Dreamdesk VR, launched their multi monitor app, allowing you to place multiple virtual screens around you in 360 degrees.

http://store.steampowered.com/app/520210
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jdhYBd61IiQ

Oh yeah, and Oculus have warned against using the Note 7 on the Gear VR headset due to it's tendency to sometimes blow up.

http://www.roadtovr.com/oculus-samsung-gear-vr-note-7-recall-warning/

DRONES

Youtube user Jopie Wise uploaded a cool video the other day showing off his custom built drone, with a frame made out of an orange box. Surprisingly it flies pretty well, and it can even carry emergency bread and pastries.

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GJZBnQw2JQU

MIXED REALITY

HoloFriends created a floor is lava game using the Microsoft Hololens. I'm definitely excited to see more applications that can transform the physical world into other things.
